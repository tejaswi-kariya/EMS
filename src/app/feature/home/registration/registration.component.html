Event Management

Event management, as described in Event Management For Dummies, is the process of planning, organizing, and executing various events, both large and small, from conferences and festivals to weddings and corporate functions. It involves managing all aspects of an event, from initial concept to post-event marketing, including budgeting, scheduling, promotion, venue selection, security, health and safety, and entertainment. Essentially, it's about turning an idea into a successful, memorable experience.
